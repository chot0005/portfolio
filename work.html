Scrapbook Project

Graphic Designer | April 2015

The Scrapbook Project serves as both an informative guide and a marketing tool, aiming to boost tourism and promote Trat province as a top destination in Thailand. By combining aesthetics with functionality, this digital booklet ensures that users can easily explore and plan their trip to Trat while enjoying an engaging and informative experience.

Challenge Trat lacks visibility in the tourism market, overshadowed by more popular destinations. The challenge is to create a compelling, user-friendly tool to attract tourists and showcase its hidden gems.

Solution A visually engaging digital scrapbook that offers interactive, easy-to-navigate content on Trat’s attractions, accommodations, and culture, emphasizing its off-the-beaten-path appeal.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator

Digital Advertising - Éclair Recipe

Graphic Designer | July 2014

Create a digital advertising campaign to promote a bakery's special Éclair, showcasing its unique recipe, flavors, and craftsmanship. The advertisement will highlight the bakery’s skill and attention to detail, attracting new customers and encouraging repeat visits by emphasizing this delectable, limited-time treat.

Challenge Despite its high-quality offerings, the bakery faces strong competition in the dessert market. The challenge is to differentiate this Éclair and generate excitement around its limited-time availability to drive traffic and sales.

Solution The solution is a targeted digital advertising campaign that showcases the Éclair's craftsmanship through engaging visuals and messaging. Using social media, email marketing, and retargeting ads, the campaign creates urgency and highlights the Éclair’s exclusivity, enticing customers to visit the bakery before it’s gone.

UNICEF Fundraising Poster

Graphic Designer | May 2014

Design a poster that supports UNICEF's campaign to raise funds for children in need. The poster should not only align with UNICEF's brand identity but also subtly incorporate Le Méridien Hotel's branding to showcase their partnership in this noble cause. The poster should aim to encourage donations, inspire action, and appeal emotionally to the audience.

Challenge The challenge is to design a poster that balances UNICEF’s humanitarian message with Le Méridien Hotel’s premium brand image, ensuring the partnership is visible yet seamless while creating a compelling call to action for donations.

Solution The solution is a visually striking poster featuring a heartfelt image of children benefiting from UNICEF's work, paired with a clear, emotionally charged message. Le Méridien’s logo will be subtly integrated in a way that supports the cause, ensuring the focus remains on the urgency of the fundraising need while showcasing the hotel’s role in the partnership.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator | Adobe Photoshop
